How is Value calculated?

The "Value Score" (0-100) uniquely measures what kind of experience you are getting for the price. It evaluates your chosen weights scaling against the custom 0-100 rubric for each ship.

🧒 Kids Score: Reflects age-appropriate programming, splash pads, and childcare capabilities.
🚢 Ship Score: Reflects the modernization, layout, crowds, and physical aesthetic of the vessel itself.
💰 Price Score: Evaluates the overall nightly pricing specifically comparing to the most expensive competing deal inside your custom filters.

🍽️ The Dining Formula

Because a massive percentage of your experience heavily leans on what you eat, the dining score dynamically shifts depending on which mode you click above:

  • Main Mode: Ranks the ship simply on its Main Dining Room (free food).
  • Package Mode: Simulates buying a specialty dining package. It maps how many 'unique' high-end restaurants the ship has (e.g. strict cap of 2 nights on Disney, up to 7 on NCL) and dynamically averages that across your cruise. If you run out of unique top-tier restaurants on a long cruise, your remaining nights fall back into the Main Dining Room!
  • Suite Mode: Accounts for suite-exclusive dining venues natively bridging the gap with your estimated suite room costs.
